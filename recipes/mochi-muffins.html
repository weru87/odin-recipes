Mochi Muffins

Back to startpage

Description

“If you want to try mochi, this is a fun recipe to start with,” says recipe developer and test kitchen expert Juliana Hale. We couldn’t agree more! This mochi muffin recipe is sweet, chewy, light, and fluffy. What’s not to love?

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mochiko
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can unsweetened coconut milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp black sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients together in one bowl and the wet ingredients in another.
  2. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture.
  3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin cups and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  4. Bake the muffins until a toothpick comes out clean.
